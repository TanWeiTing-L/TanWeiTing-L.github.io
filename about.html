About Us

We...

Keep people like you (netizens) from doing anything that'll get your information leaked.

How?

By keeping this sort of information online, sending this website to your friends and family can have them be informed about the basics of internet safety in a pinch!

We also have our own hotline at 123-456-7890 to offer direct support to individuals facing issues like cyberbullying, online scams, or identity theft.

Not only that, we're conducting research on emerging internet safety threats, such as AI-based deepfakes or sophisticated scams.

What is internet safety?

Internet safety involves practices and precautions to protect personal information and avoid online threats.

How can I protect my information?

Use strong passwords, avoid sharing personal information, and be cautious of suspicious links and emails.
