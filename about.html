About Us

Who We Are

The Internet Safety Coalition is a dedicated team of cybersecurity experts, educators, and advocates committed to promoting internet safety for individuals and communities worldwide. We aim to empower users with the knowledge and tools they need to navigate the digital world securely and confidently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safer internet for everyone by spreading awareness, educating people on cybersecurity best practices, and advocating for responsible online behavior. We believe that everyone deserves to explore the digital world without fear of fraud, theft, or exploitation.

What We Do

The Internet Safety Coalition provides a range of resources, including workshops, guides, and online tools, to help users understand the complexities of internet safety. From teaching children how to identify online scams to helping businesses protect their sensitive data, we tailor our efforts to meet the needs of diverse audiences.

Our Vision

We envision a digital future where users of all ages can interact online safely and responsibly. By fostering awareness and advocating for better security measures, we strive to make the internet a secure and positive space for everyone.

Get Involved

The Internet Safety Coalition invites individuals, educators, and organizations to join us in promoting internet safety. Whether through partnerships, volunteering, or participating in our programs, you can help us make a difference in creating a safer digital world.
